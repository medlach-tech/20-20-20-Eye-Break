Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13th, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how EyeBreak ("we," "our," or "the Extension") handles your information. We believe in "Privacy by Design" — the extension is built to function without collecting personal data.

🔒 Key takeaway: EyeBreak operates entirely on your device. We don't have servers to upload to, accounts to create, or any reason to collect your data.

1. Information We Collect

Unlike tools that require account creation, this Extension is designed to be used anonymously.

  • User-Provided Information: We do not require a username, email, or account to function. You use EyeBreak completely anonymously.
  • Local Settings: Any preferences you set (e.g., notification sounds, timer intervals, break duration) are stored locally on your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Page Content Monitoring: This extension does not read, store, or transmit the content of the websites you visit. We have zero interest in your browsing activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your local settings are used strictly to:

  • Maintain your preferred timer intervals and notification preferences across browser sessions.
  • Track your "daily streak" (if applicable) to help you monitor your eye health habits — this data is also stored locally and can be cleared at any time.

No information is used for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Server Uploads: Your timing data, settings, and streak information stay on your device. We do not operate external servers to process or store your break schedule.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any data with third parties. There is simply no data to share. We have no interest in — and no access to — your browsing habits.
  • No Third-Party Integrations: The extension does not include third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs.

4. Data Security

Since all your data remains within your Chrome browser's local environment, it is protected by Google Chrome's native security measures. No data is transmitted over the network, eliminating risks associated with data-in-transit interception.

5. Your Rights

You have full and immediate control over your data. You can reset your stats or delete all extension data at any time by:

  • Clearing the extension storage via Chrome's extension settings.
  • Uninstalling the extension, which automatically removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is a productivity and wellness tool suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13, or from any user for that matter. Since no personal information is collected, there is no risk of accidental data collection from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date. Because the extension operates entirely locally, most updates will not change how your data is handled. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
